Need to know about Spain and Italy before you visit?

Here are some tips from my experience:

Having had the pleasure of visiting iconic landmarks like Camp Nou in Barcelona, the Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy, the Colosseum in Rome, La Rambla in Barcelona, the Alhambra in Granada, and the Florence Cathedral, I'm excited to share some travel tips for these incredible destinations. When visiting Camp Nou, book a guided tour to explore the football Mecca fully. Don't forget to pay homage to the legends who've graced this field. At the Leaning Tower of Pisa, arrive early to avoid the crowds and take that classic 'holding up the tower' photo. Explore the Colosseum in the morning for fewer crowds and consider a guided tour for deeper insights. La Rambla in Barcelona is a lively promenade; try the local cafes and street food. When visiting the Alhambra, book tickets in advance and savor the Moorish architecture. Inside the Florence Cathedral, admire the stunning artwork and consider climbing the Duomo for panoramic views.
